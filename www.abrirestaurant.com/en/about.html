Who is Abri?

The concept was created in 2018 by Marion & Ludo, life partners, work partners, and food partners. Today, it's with their teams that they continue to push boundaries to always offer you the best and delight you.

What is Abri?

It's a street food restaurant with Mediterranean inspiration, but not only...
Here, we offer you an avalanche of culinary inspirations, emphasizing the taste and freshness of our products. You can choose between a pita or a bowl, and if you're hungry or just want a light snack, there are also sides to share or nibble on solo.

Where does it come from?

  • Pita bread, made by an artisan
  • Meat, sourced by Maison Milleret in Chambéry, always of French origin
  • Vegetables, fresh and as seasonal as possible
  • Sauces, always homemade and well seasoned
  • Potatoes, cut in the kitchen, double cooked and seasoned with smoked paprika
  • Hummus, always creamy, with the perfect tahini
  • In short, Abri is a friendly cuisine that stands out from the ordinary, Mediterranean flavors that rock, a cozy atmosphere, in short... come settle in at Abri, you'll see, it's good here.
